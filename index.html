Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe!

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game where players take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid. The goal is to be the first to get three of your symbols (X or O) in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

How to Play:

  1. Choose your colors for Player X and Player O.
  2. Click the "Start Game" button to begin.
  3. Select a mode: Play against the AI or another player.
  4. Player X goes first. Click on any cell to place your symbol.
  5. Alternate turns until one player wins or the game ends in a draw.
